About Me

"Get busy living, or get busy dying." Favorite quotes can say a lot about a person: who we are, where we'regoing, what'simportant to us, and, yes, even our favorite movie. If you haven't placed the quote yet, it's from the movie'Shawshank Redemption'. Now, I know that Tim Robbins was dealing with some very drastic life choices, but I've always loved thesentiment.


Now that I've gotten the most important stuff out of the way, let me tell you a little about myself.I was born Lynn Cunningham in the picturesque little town of Pinetop, Arizona. For the first few years of my life I bounced betweenArizona and Texas where my parents worked in the child-care industry. This meant my first homes were in orphanages, but unlike theother children in residence, I had both of my parents. It was a pretty unorthodox adolescence.


In 1997 I moved to Tucson. I married my husband and we formed Affordable Fence and Gates which is an ornamental iron contracting business. It started with just the two of us: my husband handled the building and installation and I managed the books. It has grown quite a bit over the years. We no longer work out of our home and we have grown to 25 employees.


I have two incredible kids. My daughter is 18 and attending U of A and my son is a 16 year old JunioratCanyon Del Oro High School. I love to scuba dive, hike, read, build our business and travel. My dream is to retire on a boat somewhere in the Mediteranian Ocean while I freelance as a web designer.
